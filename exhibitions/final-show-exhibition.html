Final Show - Foundation Diploma in Art and Design (May 2014)

Exhibition Gallery

This exhibition was the final product of my Foundation Degree at Newcastle College in which I displayed a series of wire sculptures. The sculptures I produced for this exhibition were a progression on a piece shown at the Open Studios, which was a wire sculpture face inspired Alberto Giacometti’s two-dimensional drawings. Following from a face I decided the next move would transitioning the full human form as this would help combine the inspiration of Giacometti’s two and three-dimensional works.
